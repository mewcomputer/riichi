## Design Context

### Users

Project owners and everyday issue operators use Riichi to triage work, inspect
agent state, resolve blockers, and take the next authorized action quickly.

### Brand Personality

Calm, quick, unsurprising.

### Aesthetic Direction

Dark-only, quiet and utilitarian, with strong typographic hierarchy and sparse
accent use. Existing design tokens and font roles are authoritative.

### Design Principles

- Make state and the next safe action obvious.
- Preserve queue context and avoid surprise navigation.
- Prefer clear labels, explicit feedback, and reversible actions.
- Treat keyboard, pointer, narrow screens, and reduced motion as equal paths.
- Keep the visual system restrained: no decorative gradients, glows, or card
  grids that do not improve comprehension.
